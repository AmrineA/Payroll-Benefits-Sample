Payroll & Benefits Sample Application

You will find the follow features under Employees:

  • Add/remove employees
  • Add/remove dependents to employees
  • Modify employees' names, ages and gross pay amounts
  • Modify dependents' names and ages
  • View employee pay summaries (gross pay, benefits, discounts, and net pay)

Under Organization you can do the following:

  • Modify the benefits costs for employees and dependents
  • Modify the name of the organization
  • Change the number of paychecks per year
